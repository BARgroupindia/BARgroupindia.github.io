Product Overview

Green pigment solutions comprise a comprehensive range of high-performance organic and inorganic colorants engineered to deliver precise shade control, high tinting strength, and excellent dispersion across diverse industrial systems. The portfolio includes copper phthalocyanine-based chemistries offering vibrant bluish to yellowish green tones with superior color strength and cost efficiency, alongside advanced organic variants designed for enhanced transparency and durability. Complementing these are robust inorganic compositions such as chromium oxide and complex metal oxide pigments, which provide high opacity, exceptional chemical stability, and consistent performance in demanding environments. Together, these materials enable flexible formulation across coatings, plastics, inks, construction materials, and specialty applications.

These pigments are manufactured under stringent quality controls to ensure uniform particle size distribution, reliable batch-to-batch consistency, and strong resistance to heat, light, weathering, and chemicals. High-performance grades support long-term outdoor durability and reduced maintenance cycles, while optimized dispersion characteristics allow efficient processing and lower pigment loading, improving overall formulation economics. Available in multiple supply forms — including powders, presscakes, dispersions, granules, and masterbatches — these solutions are compatible with a wide range of industrial systems. With options meeting global regulatory requirements and sustainability considerations, they provide dependable performance across both standard and high-end applications.

Pigment Green 7 (PG7)

Chlorinated copper phthalocyanine pigment offering a bluish green shade with extremely high tinting strength and excellent weatherfastness. It is the most widely used green pigment globally.

  • Bluish green shade (high chroma)
  • Very high tint strength (100–130%)
  • Excellent lightfastness & weather resistance
  • Surface-treated grades for coatings, plastics & inks
  • Available in powder, presscake, flush & dispersion forms

Applications: Plastics, coatings, inks (offset, flexo, gravure), textiles, rubber, packaging, laminates.

Pigment Green 36 (PG36)

Brominated phthalocyanine green pigment providing a yellowish green shade with higher brightness and improved transparency compared to PG7.

  • Yellowish green (bright tone)
  • High tint strength (100–125%)
  • Excellent dispersion & transparency
  • High-performance coatings grade
  • Suitable for automotive & decorative coatings

Applications: Automotive coatings, high-end plastics, inks, packaging, appliance coatings.

Pigment Green 17 (PG17)

Chromium oxide green (Cr₂O₃), an inorganic pigment known for its exceptional durability, opacity, and chemical resistance.

  • Opaque green shade
  • Excellent heat stability (500°C+)
  • Outstanding weather & alkali resistance
  • Low oil absorption
  • Micronized grades for improved dispersion

Applications: Cement, concrete, coatings, ceramics, construction materials, camouflage coatings.

Pigment Green 18 (PG18)

Hydrated chromium oxide pigment with slightly duller tone, offering strong corrosion resistance and stability in industrial environments.

  • Dull green shade
  • High chemical & corrosion resistance
  • Good weather stability
  • Stable under harsh environments

Applications: Anti-corrosion coatings, primers, industrial coatings, structural steel protection.

Pigment Green 26 (PG26)

Cobalt chromium oxide spinel pigment designed for high-temperature and high-durability applications.

  • Dark green shade
  • Excellent heat stability (500°C+)
  • High infrared reflectance (cool pigment)
  • Superior weather resistance

Applications: Coil coatings, aerospace coatings, military coatings, high-temp plastics, solar reflective coatings.

Pigment Green 50 (PG50)

Nickel titanate pigment offering a yellowish green shade with excellent durability and opacity.

  • Yellowish green tone
  • Excellent heat stability (500°C+)
  • High opacity
  • Strong weather resistance

Applications: Ceramics, glass, architectural coatings, façade coatings, roofing materials.

Pigment Green 8 (PG8)

Economical organic green pigment used in general-purpose applications where cost efficiency is critical.

  • Bluish green shade
  • Moderate tint strength
  • Good cost-performance balance
  • Suitable for non-critical applications

Applications: Decorative paints, plastics, rubber, paper, low-cost inks.

Pigment Green 10 (PG10)

Conventional organic green pigment similar to PG8 with slight variation in tone and application flexibility.

  • Bluish green shade
  • Moderate performance
  • Good dispersibility

Applications: Printing inks, coatings, plastics, packaging, stationery inks.

Pigment Green 58 (PG58)

High-performance organic green pigment with improved transparency and durability compared to conventional grades.

  • Yellowish green shade
  • High transparency
  • Good heat stability
  • Enhanced performance in coatings

Applications: High-performance coatings, inks, plastics, packaging.

Pigment Green 59 (PG59)

Specialty organic green pigment offering bright shade and improved durability for premium applications.

  • Bright green shade
  • Good weather resistance
  • Balanced tint strength
  • High-performance applications

Applications: Automotive coatings, premium coatings, specialty inks, plastics.

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Pigment Green products, including phthalocyanine greens (PG7, PG36), chromium oxide greens (PG17, PG18), and complex inorganic pigments such as cobalt and nickel-based greens (PG26, PG50), are generally non-flammable, chemically stable, and insoluble materials. However, fine powders may generate airborne dust during handling, which can cause mild mechanical irritation to the eyes, skin, and respiratory tract. Certain grades may contain metal compounds (e.g., copper, chromium, cobalt, nickel), and appropriate industrial hygiene practices should be followed.

Storage Guidelines:
  • - Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated environment
  • - Keep containers tightly sealed to prevent moisture absorption and contamination
  • - Protect from direct sunlight and extreme temperature variations
  • - Avoid contamination from incompatible materials and foreign substances
  • - Store away from strong oxidizing agents and reactive chemicals
Required PPE:
  • - Dust respirators (N95 / P2 or equivalent) in powder handling environments
  • - Safety goggles or protective eyewear
  • - Protective gloves and appropriate industrial workwear
  • - Protective clothing for prolonged or repeated exposure
Handling Precautions:
  • - Minimize dust generation during transfer, mixing, and processing operations
  • - Use local exhaust ventilation (LEV) or dust extraction systems where required
  • - Handle materials in closed or controlled systems for bulk operations
  • - Avoid direct contact with eyes and prolonged skin exposure
  • - Use vacuum cleaning systems instead of dry sweeping or compressed air
  • - Maintain good housekeeping practices to prevent dust accumulation

Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available upon request for detailed information on occupational exposure limits, regulatory compliance (REACH, RoHS), and safe handling practices.

Packaging & Transportation

Pigment Green products are supplied in multiple packaging formats depending on physical form (powder, presscake, dispersion, granules, or masterbatch) and customer handling requirements. Packaging is designed to ensure product integrity, moisture protection, and efficient handling across domestic and international supply chains.

Packaging Options:
  • Paper Bags / HDPE Bags: Typically 20–25 kg multi-wall or laminated bags with inner liners for powdered pigments
  • FIBC / Jumbo Bags: 500–1000 kg bulk bags for large-scale industrial applications
  • Fiber Drums: 25–50 kg drums for specialty, micronized, or high-purity grades
  • Liquid Dispersions: Supplied in HDPE drums or IBC tanks (200–1000 liters)
  • Plastic Masterbatch: Supplied in pellet or granule form in moisture-resistant packaging
  • Customized Packaging: Available as per customer specifications and export requirements
Palletization & Export Packing:
  • - Bags and drums are palletized on heat-treated wooden or plastic pallets (ISPM-15 compliant)
  • - Typically 40–50 bags per pallet depending on packaging configuration
  • - Stretch-wrapped and securely strapped for stability during transit
  • - Use of moisture barriers, liners, and protective covers where required
  • - Clear labeling with product name, grade, batch number, and handling instructions
Container Loading & Handling:
  • - Loaded into 20 ft / 40 ft containers as per international logistics practices
  • - Proper weight distribution maintained to prevent shifting during transit
  • - Use of dunnage materials, pallet locks, and bracing where necessary
  • - Pre-dispatch inspection conducted to ensure packaging integrity and compliance
Transportation & Logistics:
  • - Generally classified as non-hazardous for transport (subject to grade and regional regulations)
  • - Suitable for road, rail, and sea freight transportation
  • - Handled as packaged or bulk cargo under standard shipping conditions
  • - Full documentation support including COA, SDS, and export documentation
  • - Batch traceability ensured through labeling and quality control systems

Flexible packaging, labeling, and logistics solutions are available to meet specific industry requirements and customer handling systems.
