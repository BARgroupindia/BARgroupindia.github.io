Product Overview

Silicon dioxide (SiO₂) is a versatile inorganic material supplied across a broad spectrum of forms, ranging from naturally occurring crystalline minerals to highly engineered amorphous and ultra-high purity variants. These materials are available in multiple particle sizes and physical formats — including granular, micronized, colloidal dispersions, and ultrafine powders — designed to meet the performance requirements of diverse industrial processes. With controlled purity levels, surface characteristics, and structural properties, these products support applications that demand consistency, durability, and precise functional behavior across sectors such as glass manufacturing, ceramics, construction, coatings, polymers, and advanced composites.

In addition to conventional industrial uses, advanced and high-purity forms are engineered for specialized applications requiring exceptional chemical stability, optical clarity, and controlled reactivity. These materials play a critical role in high-performance systems including electronics, energy technologies, precision polishing, filtration, and environmental solutions. Their adaptability, abundance, and compatibility with modern processing technologies make them an essential component in both large-scale industrial operations and high-value specialty applications, supporting efficiency, sustainability, and product innovation across global supply chains.

Industrial Sand
(IS / IND-SAND)

High-quality silica sand with controlled grain size and excellent chemical stability, widely used across industrial manufacturing and processing applications.

  • High SiO₂ content with low impurities
  • Uniform particle distribution for consistent processing
  • Excellent hardness and thermal resistance
  • Available in multiple mesh sizes
  • Reliable performance in industrial environments

Applications: Construction materials, glass production, water filtration, foundries, and abrasive blasting.

Quartz Powder
(QP / QTZ-PWD)

Finely processed quartz powder offering superior whiteness, purity, and particle consistency for precision industrial applications.

  • High brightness and chemical purity

  • Low moisture and contamination levels
  • Suitable for high-performance industrial formulations

Applications: Ceramics, paints, coatings, engineered stone, adhesives, and refractory products.

Cristobalite
(CRIS / CTB)

Thermally processed silica material with low thermal expansion and exceptional dimensional stability for advanced industrial use.

  • Low coefficient of thermal expansion
  • High temperature resistance
  • Improved dimensional stability in ceramics
  • Optimized particle morphology
  • Excellent compatibility with resin systems

Applications: Precision casting, ceramics, fiberglass, engineered stone, and specialty coatings.

Foundry Grade
(FG / F-SiO₂)

Premium silica material engineered for metal casting operations, delivering high thermal stability and mold integrity.

  • Excellent refractoriness and heat resistance

  • Low clay and moisture content

  • Consistent casting surface finish

Applications: Iron casting, steel foundries, non-ferrous metal molding, and core manufacturing.

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Silicon dioxide (SiO₂), including crystalline silica (quartz, cristobalite) and amorphous forms (precipitated, fumed, colloidal silica), is widely used across industrial applications. Respirable crystalline silica dust may pose health risks if inhaled over prolonged periods, potentially leading to lung-related conditions. Fine powders and ultrafine silica (fumed / micronized) may also cause eye irritation, skin dryness, and respiratory discomfort. Proper dust control, ventilation systems, and adherence to occupational safety guidelines are essential during handling, processing, and storage.

Storage Guidelines:
  • - Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated environment (recommended ambient conditions)
  • - Keep containers tightly sealed to prevent moisture ingress and contamination
  • - Protect from excessive humidity, water exposure, and environmental contaminants
  • - Avoid segregation issues by storing different particle sizes and grades separately
  • - Store away from strong alkalis (for reactive silica forms) and incompatible materials
Required PPE:
  • - Dust respirators (N95 / FFP2 or higher) for powder handling
  • - Safety goggles to prevent eye irritation from fine particles
  • - Protective gloves to avoid skin dryness or abrasion
  • - Protective clothing in high dust exposure environments
Handling Precautions:
  • - Minimize dust generation and avoid inhalation of respirable particles
  • - Use local exhaust ventilation (LEV) or dust collection systems
  • - Prefer enclosed conveying systems for bulk material handling
  • - Avoid direct eye contact and prolonged skin exposure
  • - Use wet cleaning or industrial vacuum systems instead of dry sweeping
  • - Maintain housekeeping to prevent accumulation of fine silica dust

Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available upon request, including information on occupational exposure limits (OSHA, ACGIH), safe handling practices, and regulatory compliance.

Packaging & Transportation

Silicon dioxide products are supplied in various packaging formats depending on particle size, form (powder, granules, beads, or liquid dispersion), and end-use applications. Packaging systems are designed to ensure moisture protection, product integrity, and safe handling across industrial supply chains.

Packaging Options:
  • Paper Bags / HDPE Bags: Typically 25 kg laminated bags for silica powder and sand grades
  • FIBC / Jumbo Bags: 500–1500 kg bulk packaging for industrial silica sand and quartz
  • Fiber Drums: 25–50 kg drums for high-purity or specialty silica (fumed / micronized)
  • Liquid Forms: Colloidal silica supplied in HDPE drums or IBC tanks (200–1000 liters)
  • Granular / Beaded Forms: Silica gel and desiccant grades in moisture-resistant packaging
  • Low-Dust Grades: Engineered forms to reduce airborne particles during handling
  • Customized Packaging: Available as per customer specifications and export requirements
Palletization & Export Packing:
  • - Bags and drums palletized on wooden or plastic pallets (ISPM-15 compliant)
  • - Typically 40–50 units per pallet depending on packaging type
  • - Stretch-wrapped and strapped for stability during transportation
  • - Moisture barriers and liners used for hygroscopic or fine grades
  • - Proper labeling with grade, batch number, and handling instructions
Container Loading & Handling:
  • - Loaded into 20 ft / 40 ft containers as per international logistics standards
  • - Even weight distribution to prevent shifting and damage
  • - Use of dunnage and securing systems where required
  • - Pre-shipment inspection ensures packaging integrity
Transportation & Logistics:
  • - Generally classified as non-hazardous (depending on form and SDS classification)
  • - Suitable for road, rail, sea, and air transport
  • - Handled under standard bulk and packaged cargo conditions
  • - Documentation support including COA, SDS, and technical datasheets
  • - Full batch traceability maintained for quality assurance

Flexible packaging and logistics solutions are available to meet diverse industrial and export requirements.

Recycling & Sustainability

Silicon dioxide plays a vital role in sustainable industrial processes across construction, glass, rubber, filtration, and electronics sectors due to its abundance, recyclability, and durability.

  • - Silica sand and glass-grade materials are recyclable and support circular glass manufacturing
  • - Used in water filtration systems, improving water quality and environmental protection
  • - Enhances durability of construction materials, reducing lifecycle environmental impact
  • - Precipitated silica improves fuel efficiency in tires by reducing rolling resistance
  • - Non-toxic and chemically stable material suitable for long-term applications
  • - Supports energy-efficient technologies (solar panels, semiconductors)
  • - Engineered silica enables reduced material usage and improved process efficiency
