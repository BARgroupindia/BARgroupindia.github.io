Product Overview

Carbon black is a highly engineered form of elemental carbon produced through controlled thermal decomposition of hydrocarbon feedstocks. It is widely used as a functional additive across rubber, plastics, coatings, inks, and advanced material applications. Its performance is defined by key parameters such as particle size, surface area, structure, and surface chemistry, which can be precisely tailored to deliver reinforcement, pigmentation, conductivity, and UV protection. In elastomer systems, it enhances tensile strength, abrasion resistance, and durability, while in polymer and coating applications it provides deep coloration, weatherability, and long-term stability.

Beyond conventional uses, carbon black also plays a critical role in high-performance and specialty applications requiring electrical conductivity, purity control, and regulatory compliance. It is utilized in energy storage systems, electronic components, and sensitive applications where consistency and controlled impurity levels are essential. With advancements in process technology and sustainable practices, carbon black continues to evolve as a versatile material supporting efficiency, product longevity, and environmental objectives across a wide range of industrial sectors.

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Carbon black is a fine particulate material that may generate airborne dust during handling. Prolonged inhalation of dust may cause respiratory irritation. Dust clouds may form explosive mixtures in air under certain conditions; therefore, proper dust control and grounding measures must be implemented.

Storage Guidelines:
  • - Store in a dry, cool, and well-ventilated environment
  • - Protect from moisture ingress to maintain product flowability and dispersion quality
  • - Avoid contamination from foreign materials or chemicals
  • - Store away from strong oxidizing agents
  • - Use dedicated silos or sealed containers for bulk storage
Required PPE:
  • - Dust respirators (P2 / P3 or NIOSH-approved particulate filters)
  • - Safety goggles or face protection
  • - Protective gloves and industrial workwear
  • - Anti-static safety footwear in dust-prone environments
Handling Precautions:
  • - Use enclosed conveying systems (pneumatic or mechanical) to minimize dust emissions
  • - Provide local exhaust ventilation (LEV) at transfer and mixing points
  • - Avoid use of compressed air for cleaning; use vacuum systems instead
  • - Implement grounding and bonding to prevent static charge buildup
  • - Prevent accumulation of dust layers on surfaces and equipment
  • - Ensure proper housekeeping and dust management practices

Safety Data Sheet (SDS) available upon request for detailed occupational exposure limits and regulatory compliance.

Packaging & Transportation

Carbon black is supplied in various packaging formats depending on grade (powder or pelletized), handling systems, and customer requirements. Packaging is designed to ensure dust control, product integrity, and efficient logistics across domestic and international supply chains.

Packaging Options:
  • Paper Bags / HDPE Bags: Typically 20–25 kg multi-wall bags for powdered grades with inner liners to prevent moisture ingress
  • FIBC / Jumbo Bags: 500–1000 kg bulk bags with anti-static options for safe handling and storage
  • Pelletized Carbon Black Bags: Low-dust granules packed in bags or bulk containers for automated feeding systems
  • Bulk Tanker / Silo Supply: Pneumatic tankers for large-scale industrial users with direct silo feeding
  • Customized Packaging: Available based on customer specifications and export requirements
Palletization & Export Packing:
  • - Bags are palletized on heat-treated wooden or plastic pallets (ISPM-15 compliant for export)
  • - Typically 40–50 bags per pallet depending on configuration
  • - Stretch-wrapped and strapped to ensure load stability
  • - Use of anti-slip sheets and protective covers for moisture protection
  • - Clear labeling with product grade, batch number, and handling instructions
Container Loading & Handling:
  • - Loaded into 20 ft / 40 ft containers as per standard stuffing norms
  • - Proper weight distribution to prevent shifting during transit
  • - Use of dunnage and bracing materials where required
  • - Pre-dispatch inspection to ensure packaging integrity
Transportation & Logistics:
  • - Typically classified as non-hazardous for transport (varies by region and form)
  • - Handled as dry bulk or packaged cargo under standard freight conditions
  • - Suitable for road, rail, and sea transport
  • - End-to-end logistics support including documentation (COA, SDS, shipping papers)
  • - Traceability maintained through batch identification and labeling

Tailored packaging, labeling, and logistics solutions are available based on industry requirements and customer-specific handling systems.

Recycling & Sustainability

Our carbon black portfolio supports circular economy initiatives through efficient material utilization, product design, and integration of sustainable alternatives across multiple industries.

  • - Recovered Carbon Black (rCB) can be incorporated in tyre grade and MRG applications for partial replacement of virgin furnace carbon black
  • - Pigment, coating, and ink grade carbon blacks enable durable coloration with extended product life and reduced repainting cycles
  • - Conductive and acetylene blacks support energy-efficient applications such as lithium-ion batteries and electronic components
  • - UV stabilized carbon black enhances product lifespan in plastics, pipes, and outdoor applications by preventing degradation
  • - Masterbatch solutions improve material efficiency, reduce dust handling, and optimize processing in polymer applications
  • - Low PAH and high purity carbon blacks support regulatory compliance in sensitive applications such as food-contact, cosmetics, and medical use
  • - Optimized feedstock utilization and process efficiency contribute to reduced emissions and improved sustainability performance
