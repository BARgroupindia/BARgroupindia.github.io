Product Overview

Soda Ash (Sodium Carbonate) is a versatile inorganic alkali widely utilized across core industrial sectors for its consistent alkalinity, buffering capacity, and reactivity. It plays a critical role in glass manufacturing as a primary flux, while also serving as an essential builder in detergent and soap formulations, enabling effective cleaning and water softening. In chemical processing, it functions as a key intermediate and pH regulator, supporting a broad range of synthesis and neutralization reactions. Its application further extends to water treatment for hardness removal and pH adjustment, textile processing for dye fixation and scouring, pulp and paper manufacturing for pulping and bleaching operations, and metallurgical processes where it aids in impurity removal and fluxing. With reliable performance across these industries, it remains a foundational chemical for large-scale and precision-driven operations alike.

Engineered to meet diverse operational requirements, this product is available in multiple physical forms and compositions that ensure optimal handling, solubility, and process efficiency across applications. Variants range from free-flowing particulate forms designed for bulk handling and automated dosing systems to fine, rapidly dissolving forms suited for high-reactivity environments, as well as hydrated and liquid formats that enable direct integration into continuous processing systems. High-purity and refined variants are developed with strict impurity control for sensitive applications, including optical, food, and pharmaceutical use, ensuring compliance with global quality standards. Additionally, it is sourced through both naturally derived mineral routes and controlled synthetic processes, offering flexibility in sustainability, consistency, and supply reliability while maintaining uniform chemical performance across applications.

Dense Soda Ash

High bulk density sodium carbonate grade engineered for glass manufacturing and heavy industrial processes, offering superior handling, reduced dust formation, and optimized furnace performance.

  • High bulk density (0.9 – 1.1 g/cm³)
  • Low dust formation for improved handling
  • Uniform particle size distribution
  • Excellent flowability and storage stability
  • High chemical purity for consistent melting behavior

Applications: Glass manufacturing (flat & container glass), silicate production, detergents, metallurgy, and chemical processing industries.

Light Soda Ash

Fine, low-density sodium carbonate grade designed for applications requiring rapid solubility, enhanced reactivity, and uniform dispersion across aqueous systems.

  • Low bulk density (0.4 – 0.6 g/cm³)
  • Fast dissolution and high reactivity
  • Fine particle structure for uniform mixing
  • Consistent alkalinity control
  • Suitable for precision chemical formulations

Applications: Detergents, soaps, water treatment, chemical synthesis, textile processing, and paper industries.

Medium Density Soda Ash

Balanced-density sodium carbonate grade offering optimized flowability and solubility, bridging the performance gap between light and dense variants for versatile industrial usage.

  • Moderate bulk density for controlled handling
  • Improved solubility compared to dense grade
  • Reduced dust compared to light grade
  • Consistent particle morphology
  • Enhanced process adaptability

Applications: General chemical manufacturing, detergent blending, water softening, and industrial cleaning formulations.

Granular / Compacted Soda Ash

Mechanically densified sodium carbonate grade produced through compaction technology, ensuring superior granule strength, minimal dusting, and excellent handling characteristics.

  • Granulated structure with high mechanical strength
  • Low friability and minimal dust generation
  • Uniform particle size for automated dosing systems
  • Enhanced storage and transport stability
  • Controlled dissolution profile

Applications: Detergent manufacturing, bulk handling systems, chemical dosing, and industrial cleaning compounds.

Refined / Optical Grade

Ultra-high purity sodium carbonate grade with strict impurity control, specifically developed for optical glass and specialty applications requiring exceptional clarity and minimal trace contaminants.

  • Very low iron and heavy metal content

  • Suitable for precision-grade manufacturing

Applications: Optical glass, specialty glass, electronics, laboratory reagents, and high-end chemical synthesis.

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Sodium carbonate (Na₂CO₃), commonly known as soda ash, is a strongly alkaline inorganic compound widely used in glass, detergents, chemicals, and water treatment. Direct contact with concentrated solutions or dust may cause irritation to eyes, skin, and respiratory tract. Prolonged exposure to airborne dust can lead to respiratory discomfort and mucous membrane irritation. Due to its alkaline nature, soda ash solutions may cause skin dryness or mild burns under extended exposure. Proper ventilation, dust control systems, and adherence to occupational safety guidelines are essential during handling and processing.

Storage Guidelines:
  • - Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated area away from moisture
  • - Keep containers tightly sealed to prevent caking and contamination
  • - Protect from humidity and water exposure due to hygroscopic nature
  • - Avoid contact with acids and incompatible materials
  • - Store different grades separately to prevent cross-contamination
Required PPE:
  • - Dust respirators (N95 / FFP2 or higher) for powder handling
  • - Safety goggles or face shields to prevent eye irritation
  • - Chemical-resistant gloves to protect from alkaline exposure
  • - Protective clothing in bulk handling or dusty environments
Handling Precautions:
  • - Minimize dust generation and avoid inhalation of fine particles
  • - Use local exhaust ventilation (LEV) or dust extraction systems
  • - Prefer enclosed systems for bulk transfer and conveying
  • - Avoid direct contact with eyes and prolonged skin exposure
  • - Use wet cleaning methods or industrial vacuum systems instead of dry sweeping
  • - Maintain proper housekeeping to prevent dust accumulation

Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available upon request, including occupational exposure limits, safe handling practices, and regulatory compliance guidelines.
