Product Overview

Pigment Blue products represent a versatile class of high-performance colorants widely utilized across coatings, plastics, printing inks, textiles, construction, and specialty applications. This portfolio includes both organic and inorganic chemistries, offering a broad spectrum of shades ranging from reddish to greenish blue, along with tailored properties such as high tinting strength, controlled particle size, excellent dispersion, and consistent color development. These pigments are engineered to deliver reliable performance across diverse processing conditions, including solvent-based, water-based, and polymer systems.

Designed to meet demanding industrial requirements, these pigments exhibit strong lightfastness, weather resistance, and chemical stability, ensuring long-term durability in both indoor and outdoor applications. Select grades provide enhanced heat resistance for engineering plastics and high-temperature coatings, while others offer cost-effective solutions for general-purpose use. With availability in multiple forms such as powders, presscakes, dispersions, and masterbatches, the portfolio supports efficient processing, regulatory compliance, and sustainable material utilization across global supply chains.

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Pigment Blue products, including phthalocyanine (PB15 series), indanthrone (PB60), and inorganic pigments such as cobalt and iron-based blues, are generally non-flammable, chemically stable, and insoluble materials. However, fine powders may generate airborne dust during handling, which can cause mild mechanical irritation to the eyes, skin, and respiratory tract. Certain grades may contain metal complexes (e.g., copper, cobalt, iron), and appropriate industrial hygiene practices should be followed.

Storage Guidelines:
  • - Store in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated environment
  • - Keep containers tightly sealed to prevent moisture absorption and contamination
  • - Protect from direct sunlight and extreme temperature variations
  • - Avoid contamination from incompatible materials and foreign substances
  • - Store away from strong oxidizing agents and reactive chemicals
Required PPE:
  • - Dust respirators (N95 / P2 or equivalent) in powder handling environments
  • - Safety goggles or protective eyewear
  • - Protective gloves and appropriate industrial workwear
  • - Protective clothing for prolonged or repeated exposure
Handling Precautions:
  • - Minimize dust generation during transfer, mixing, and processing operations
  • - Use local exhaust ventilation (LEV) or dust extraction systems where required
  • - Handle materials in closed or controlled systems for bulk operations
  • - Avoid direct contact with eyes and prolonged skin exposure
  • - Use vacuum cleaning systems instead of dry sweeping or compressed air
  • - Maintain good housekeeping practices to prevent dust accumulation

Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available upon request for detailed information on occupational exposure limits, regulatory compliance (REACH, RoHS), and safe handling practices.





Packaging & Transportation

Pigment Blue products are supplied in multiple packaging formats depending on physical form (powder, presscake, dispersion, or masterbatch) and customer handling requirements. Packaging is designed to ensure product integrity, moisture protection, and efficient handling across domestic and international supply chains.

Packaging Options:
  • Paper Bags / HDPE Bags: Typically 20–25 kg multi-wall or laminated bags with inner liners for powdered pigments
  • FIBC / Jumbo Bags: 500–1000 kg bulk bags for large-scale industrial usage
  • Fiber Drums: 25–50 kg drums for high-purity, specialty, or export-grade pigments
  • Liquid Dispersions: Supplied in HDPE drums or IBC tanks (200–1000 liters)
  • Plastic Masterbatch: Supplied in pellet form packed in moisture-resistant bags
  • Customized Packaging: Available as per customer specifications and export requirements
Palletization & Export Packing:
  • - Bags and drums are palletized on heat-treated wooden or plastic pallets (ISPM-15 compliant)
  • - Typically 40–50 bags per pallet depending on weight and configuration
  • - Stretch-wrapped and securely strapped for load stability
  • - Use of moisture barriers, liners, and protective covers where required
  • - Clear labeling with product name, grade, batch number, and handling instructions
Container Loading & Handling:
  • - Loaded into 20 ft / 40 ft containers as per standard logistics practices
  • - Proper weight distribution maintained to prevent shifting during transit
  • - Use of dunnage materials and bracing where necessary
  • - Pre-dispatch inspection conducted to ensure packaging integrity and compliance
Transportation & Logistics:
  • - Generally classified as non-hazardous for transport (subject to grade and regulatory region)
  • - Suitable for road, rail, and sea transportation
  • - Handled as packaged or bulk cargo under standard freight conditions
  • - Full documentation support including COA, SDS, and shipping documents
  • - Batch traceability ensured through labeling and quality control systems

Flexible packaging, labeling, and logistics solutions are available to meet specific industry requirements and customer handling systems.
